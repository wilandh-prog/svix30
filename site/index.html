Volatility term structure

Per listed expiration, latest snapshot. The variance-swap strip is sensitive to how far the quoted wings reach; ATM IV is the smoother reference.

Methodology. SVIX30 replicates the CBOE VIX calculation on OMXS30 options listed on Nasdaq Stockholm: forwards are estimated per expiration via put-call parity, model-free variance is computed from out-of-the-money bid/ask mid-quotes, and the two expirations bracketing 30 days are interpolated in total variance to a constant 30-day horizon. Quotes are 15-minute-delayed pre-trade data from Nasdaq Nordic trade reports. This is an unofficial research index, not investment advice.
